## Recent corrections (2026-05-21 pass)

- **LkSG § 24 Bußgeldhöhe** — skill previously said "8 Mio. EUR". Actual ceiling under § 24 Abs. 2 is **800,000 EUR** (or 2 % turnover only for entities over 400 Mio. EUR average annual turnover and only for specific Abhilfeverstöße). Corrected.
- **KSchG § 23 + Leiharbeitnehmer** — skill said "außer Betracht". Correct per BAG 2013 (Az. 2 AZR 140/12) is that Leiharbeitnehmer count at the user-Betrieb if their use is based on regelmäßigem Beschäftigungsbedarf. Corrected.
- **KI-VO timeline** — specific dates added (02.02.2025, 02.05.2025, 02.08.2025, 02.08.2026, 02.08.2027) plus 02.08.2030 milestones for public authorities.
- **CSRD Omnibus** — Feb 2025 "Stop-the-Clock" proposal, November 2024 merger plan with CSDDD and EU Taxonomy, November 2025 EP amendments. Skill now hedges accordingly.
- **HinSchG amendment** — last amended 02.12.2025 (BGBl. 2025 I Nr. 301). HinSchGOWiZustV v. 09.04.2025: Bundesamt für Justiz is the verfolgungs-Behörde.

## Verification path to "I'd use this on a real Mandat"

1. Open the area you need (e.g. `arbeitsrecht/`).
2. Read the SKILL.md. Identify every `[unverifiziert]` citation.
3. Verify each in Beck-Online or juris (~30 sec each). Open a PR removing the marker.
4. Run the skill against an anonymized historical Mandat you already know.
5. Wire a § 203-compliant gateway.
6. Use on live Mandate only after 1-5 plus your own Berufsrechts-Freigabe.
